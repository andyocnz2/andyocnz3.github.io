Success Stories




Property Advisers


Challenge

A property advisory firm struggled with efficiently tracking lease agreements, rental income, and client reporting. They needed a streamlined process to manage and update lease terms and enable real-time collaboration.

Solution

Built a comprehensive Power BI report connected to their property management system to track leases and rental income. Integrated Power Apps with Power BI to provide real-time updates, allowing agents to access and update lease contract details directly from their mobile devices during contract negotiations and renewals.

Result

The firm achieved a reduction in time spent managing lease contracts and saw significant improvements in data accuracy. Client satisfaction rose, and operational efficiency increased by 15%, with real-time updates boosting transparency and trust with clients.

FX Traders


Challenge

A Forex trader faced difficulties tracking the real-time performance of their algorithmic trading strategies across multiple portfolios, as their trading platform lacked comprehensive reporting capabilities.

Solution

Developed a Power BI dashboard to track real-time profits and performance of different algorithmic trading portfolios. Integrated data sources from the trading platform and external APIs to pull trading metrics, enabling continuous monitoring and quick analysis.

Result

The trader gained the ability to track and optimize portfolio performance in real time, leading to a increase in profit efficiency. The solution reduced manual reporting time and enhanced decision-making speed, resulting in more strategic trades and improved overall portfolio management.

Health and Safety Compliance


Challenge

Companies struggled to manage and report on health and safety data effectively, leading to delays in compliance reporting and challenges in identifying safety trends.

Solution

Created a Power BI dashboard that integrates data from various health and safety systems, providing daily tracking and reporting. The dashboard allowed companies to monitor incidents, compliance status, and safety metrics, making it easier to identify risks and take corrective actions.

Result

Companies saw a reduction in time spent preparing health and safety reports. The dashboard improved compliance and reporting accuracy by 25%, enhanced safety awareness, and led to a 15% decrease in workplace incidents over six months.

E-commerce Store


Challenge

E-Shop, a growing e-commerce platform, faced difficulties understanding customer behavior. With thousands of products and daily transactions, they needed insights to optimize sales, marketing campaigns, and product placements.

Solution

A business intelligence tool was employed to analyze customer buying patterns and preferences, segment customers for targeted marketing campaigns, track website user flow to optimize the shopping experience, and provide real-time sales analytics.

Result

E-Shop witnessed a 30% increase in sales, a 45% improvement in marketing campaign effectiveness, and a 20% reduction in cart abandonment rates.

A survey company


Challenge

A survey company needed an efficient way to deliver and compare pre- and post-survey results for clients, ensuring insights were accessible and actionable.

Solution

Developed a Power BI report that consolidates pre- and post-survey data into an interactive dashboard. This enabled clients to easily compare results, visualize trends, and gain actionable insights.

Result

Clients experienced a reduction in time spent analyzing survey data. The enhanced reporting tool increased client satisfaction by 20% and boosted client retention due to more insightful, data-driven decisions.
