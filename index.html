Case study

Retail Chain's Inventory Optimization with Power BI

Challenge:

A retailer with over 200 stores nationwide faced challenges in inventory management.
Overstocking led to increased holding costs and understocking resulted in missed sales opportunities.
There was a need for real-time insights into sales, stock levels, and demand forecasts.

Objectives:

Develop a comprehensive Power BI dashboard that would provide actionable insights for better inventory management,
optimizing stock levels across all stores, and improving sales turnover.

Solution:

Data Integration: Consolidated data from various sources: Point of Sale systems, Warehouse Management, and historical sales data.
Dashboard Design: Developed a multi-layered dashboard showing real-time stock levels, sales trends, and predictive analytics for demand forecasting.
User Training: Trained MegaMart's staff on dashboard utilization, helping them make data-driven decisions on-the-fly.

Features of the Dashboard:

Real-time Inventory Monitoring: Enabled stores to monitor stock levels and reorder proactively.
Demand Forecasting: Predictive analytics provided insights into future demand, helping stores prepare for peak sales periods.
Sales Trend Analysis: Identified best-selling products, helping stores optimize shelf space.

Results:

Stockouts reduced by 40%, ensuring customers always found what they needed.
Overstock costs decreased by 35%, improving the bottom line.
Improved sales turnover by 20% due to optimized inventory and shelf space utilization.

Feedback:

"This Power BI solution transformed our inventory management, making it more responsive and cost-effective. We now make smarter stocking decisions."
